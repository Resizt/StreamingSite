About Me

Hi, I’m Genesis Jackson, I’m a graduate from Brooklyn College and I majored in Computer Science. My main goal in life is to become a Game Developer and I’ve had this burning passion ever since I was a little child. Being inspired by series like Kingdom Hearts, Pokemon and Halo, I want to be able to give that same astronomical experience back. I was born in New Jersey but raised in Queens, New York, I’ve learned a lot about the world and my surroundings. When I enrolled into Thomas Edison CTE Highschool, I was introduced to my first Computer Science class where I learned the basics of coding in HTML, JavaScript, CSS, and a bit of python. In 2015 when I enrolled into Queensborough Community College, I started to learn C, C#, and C++. Early 2016, I transferred to Brooklyn College where I started learning C++ in-depth and I started studying various game engines that are commonly being used, like Unreal Engine and Unity. Throughout my time at Brooklyn College, my skills as a coder increased drastically. In 2020 I graduated from Brooklyn College with a B.S. Computer Science degree. From the time I’ve graduated up till now, I’m currently working on a game project called “Kindle” in Unreal Engine. A game that I’m currently directing. On my free time I like to stream on twitch and show my perspective on video games. My viewers tends to like the way how I approach, perceive games and my takes on it.
